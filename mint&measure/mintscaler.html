How to Scale Your Recipe

  1. Enter Amount: Type the quantity from your original recipe (e.g., 200g of flour).
  2. Choose a Mode:
    • By Yield: If the recipe serves 2 but you have 4 guests.
    • Quick Multiplier: Instantly double (2x) or halve (0.5x) the amount.
    • By Ingredient: If you only have 3 eggs but the recipe calls for 5, use this to scale the rest of the ingredients to match (e.g. 6g of flower).
  3. View Result: The new amount appears instantly in green at the bottom.
